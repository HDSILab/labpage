研究方向 Research Directions

利用新型数据理解公共卫生 Understanding Public Health from Novel Data

我们深入探索来自移动设备、可穿戴设备、应用程序、互联网平台以及遥感技术等多样化数据源， 构建先进的数据分析模型，以实时洞察公共卫生事件、疾病传播模式、人群健康行为和社会心理趋势。 我们的目标是为疾病预防、健康管理和政策制定提供及时、精准的数据支持。 We leverage data streams from novel sources (mobile devices, wearables, apps, the web, remote sensing) to build advanced analytical models for real-time monitoring, analysis, and prediction of public health trends, disease outbreaks, and health behaviors. Our goal is to provide timely, precise data support for disease prevention and policy making.

用人工智能赋能医疗服务 Empowering Healthcare with AI

我们专注于开发和实施尖端的人工智能解决方案，以革新医疗诊断、治疗规划和患者管理。 利用深度学习、自然语言处理和计算机视觉技术，我们的研究旨在实现疾病的早期精准识别、 个性化治疗方案的制定，以及提升医疗流程的智能化与效率，最终改善患者的健康结果。 We focus on developing and deploying advanced AI algorithms (including deep learning, NLP, and computer vision) for revolutionizing medical diagnosis, prognosis prediction, personalized treatment planning, and optimizing hospital efficiency, leading to smarter healthcare delivery.

AI科学家加速健康研究 Accelerating Health Research with AI Scientist

我们致力于构建和利用“AI科学家”平台，它通过集成大型语言模型（LLM）、知识图谱和自动化推理引擎， 模拟和辅助科学研究的各个环节。从自动化文献综述、生成创新假设、设计虚拟实验到分析复杂数据， 我们的“AI科学家”旨在大幅缩短研发周期，加速在健康和生命科学领域的突破性发现。 We build the "AI Scientist" platform, utilizing Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) to automate scientific discovery workflows, including literature review, hypothesis generation, experimental design, and data interpretation, significantly accelerating breakthroughs in health and medical fields.
