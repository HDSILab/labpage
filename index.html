研究方向 Research Directions

利用新型数据理解公共卫生 Understanding Public Health from Novel Data

我们深入探索来自移动设备、可穿戴设备、应用程序、互联网平台以及卫星遥感等多样化数据源， 构建先进的数据分析模型，以实时洞察公共卫生事件、疾病传播模式、人群健康行为和社会心理趋势。 我们的目标是为疾病预防、健康管理和政策制定提供及时、精准的数据支持。 We leverage data streams from novel sources (mobile devices, wearables, apps, the web, remote sensing) to build advanced analytical models for real-time monitoring, analysis, and prediction of public health trends, disease outbreaks, and health behaviors. Our goal is to provide timely, precise data support for disease prevention and policy making.

人工智能赋能医疗健康服务 Empowering Healthcare with AI

我们专注于开发和实施尖端的人工智能解决方案，以革新医疗诊断、治疗规划和健康管理。 利用多模态深度学习、自然语言处理等技术，我们的研究旨在实现疾病的早期精准识别、 个性化治疗方案的制定，以及提升医疗流程的智能化与效率，最终改善患者的健康结果。 We focus on developing and deploying advanced AI algorithms (including multi-modal deep learning, NLP, and computer vision) for revolutionizing medical diagnosis, prognosis prediction, personalized treatment planning, and optimizing hospital efficiency, leading to smarter healthcare delivery.

AI科学家加速健康研究 Accelerating Health Research with AI Scientist

我们致力于构建和利用“AI科学家”平台，它通过集成大型语言模型（LLM）、知识图谱和自动化推理引擎， 模拟和辅助科学研究的各个环节。从自动化文献综述、生成创新假设、设计虚拟实验到分析复杂数据， 我们的“AI科学家”旨在大幅缩短研发周期，加速在健康和生命科学领域的突破性发现。 We build the "AI Scientist" platform, utilizing Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) to automate scientific discovery workflows, including literature review, hypothesis generation, experimental design, and data interpretation, significantly accelerating breakthroughs in health and medical fields.

助理教授 / 博士生导师, 万科公共卫生与健康学院, 清华大学 Assistant Professor / Ph.D. Supervisor, Vanke School of Public Health, Tsinghua University

夏彤博士的研究以健康大数据为基础，以人工智能技术为核心驱动力，旨在通过智能化方法促进健康领域的发展。她的研究方向涵盖：基于移动与可穿戴设备数据的健康洞察分析、面向健康预测的可信机器学习方法、支持健康推理的基础模型与大模型智能体，以及加速科研工作的 AI 智能体系统等。 此前，她在武汉大学电子信息学院获得学士学位，在清华大学电子工程系获得硕士学位（师从李勇教授），并在剑桥大学计算机系取得博士学位并开展博士后研究（师从英国皇家工程院院士 Cecilia Mascolo 教授）。其研究成果发表在 NeurIPS、KDD、npj Digital Medicine 等人工智能顶级国际会议和数字健康领域权威期刊上，相关工作曾被 BBC、CGTN 等多家国际主流媒体报道。她现任中国计算机学会普适计算专委会执行委员、IEEE Pervasive Computing 编委以及 Frontiers in Digital Health 副主编，并入选国家高层次青年人才计划。 Dr. Tong Xia‘s research is grounded in large-scale health data, driven by advanced AI technologies, and aimed at empowering health and well-being. Her work spans several key areas, including health insights derived from mobile and wearable sensor data, trustworthy machine learning for health prediction, foundation models and large-model agents for health reasoning, and AI agent systems designed to accelerate scientific research. She received her Bachelor’s degree from the School of Electronic Information at Wuhan University, her Master’s degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University (supervised by Prof. Yong Li), and her Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Cambridge, where she also conducted postdoctoral research under the supervision of Prof. Cecilia Mascolo, Fellow of the Royal Academy of Engineering. Her research has been published in leading AI conferences and top digital health journals, including NeurIPS, KDD, and npj Digital Medicine, and has been featured by major international media outlets such as BBC and CGTN. She currently serves as an Executive Committee Member of the CCF Pervasive Computing Technical Committee, an Editorial Board Member of IEEE Pervasive Computing, and an Associate Editor of Frontiers in Digital Health. She has been selected for the National High-Level Young Talent Program.
